Small Point Cafe | Research

Design is really about people; the more you understand humans, the better you will be as a designer. Yaron Schoen

CURRENT SITE

I first looked at the current website that Small Point uses. It appears to be a Squarespace site but whoever created it didn't have the user experience in mind. The home page and content pages are completely different designs and the website overall lacks a good flow.

COMPETITORS

I decided to look to the competition for inspiration. I found that PVDonuts and Dave's Coffee have great websites that show off the shops very well. I noted that they are very image heavy sites and have a very cohesive aesthetic.
