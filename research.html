OTHER BLOGS

Sprouting Photographer

When searching for similar photo blogs the first one i found was Sprouting Photographer. It has a strong visual appeal, the site is very pretty. However, it's a little awkward to navigate at times. You don't always know how to get back to where you last were becuase the pages all look pretty different. The content on the site is very well written though. They souorce their articles from many different photographers. The content is also broken up with subheads that cut the article into smaller more digestable chunks.

Chase Jarvis' Blog

Chase Jarvis has always been one of my favorite photographers and greatest inspirations in life. His blog is great, he updates it very frequently with original articles, videos, and segments from his podcast. Each entry on the blog has a cover photo, an eye-catching headline and a small summary about what's inside.

Flak Photo

This last blog, Flak Photo, is a great example of a photography first blog. It puts the photos above all else. You click an article, and all of the photos referenced are right at the top for easy viewing. The rest of the design of the site is weak, large walls of small text and an outdated look and feel. The content, however, is well written and photographs are integrated nicely.
